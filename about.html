About Us

Who We Are

Supplinex Solutions Ltd is a Kenyan-owned company specializing in general supplies, professional Cleaning, fumigation and pest control services. We serve businesses, homes, institutions and government offices with a commitment to quality, reliability and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted provider of supply and facility services across East Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, timely and ecofriendly solutions that meet our clients' diverse needs in supplies, hygiene and pest management.

Why Choose Us?

  • Qualified and experienced team
  • Licensed pest control operators
  • Timely delivery of quality supplies
  • Reliable customer support
  • Custom service packages
